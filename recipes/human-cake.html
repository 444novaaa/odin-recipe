Human Cake

Description

This simple cake recipe would be perfect for Weddings, Anniversaries, and to celebrate special occasions. This cake is full of love and support, and will warm the hearts of anyone consuming it!

Ingredients

  • 6 Eggs
  • 4 Cups Milk
  • Stick of Butter
  • 2 Packs Cake Mix
  • Cigarette Butts
  • Saliva from VSauce
  • Urine from JoeySalads
  • Chewed Banana from JonTron
  • Pubic Hair from Jacksepticeye
  • Pubic Hair from KSI
  • Butt Hair from Pewdiepie
  • Toenail Clippings from Anthony Fantano
  • Toenail Clippings from Makemebad35
  • 3 Baking Pans. (One small, medium, and large.)
  • Mixing Bowl
  • Frosting
  • Sprinkles

Steps

  1. Grab your mixing bowl and crack in 6 eggs.
  2. Take your stick of butter and rip it half with your bare hands. Drop one half into the bowl.
  3. Measure out 4 cups of milk and guzzle it down.
  4. Regurgitate it into the bowl. (Make sure you vomit out at least 2 cups worth.)
  5. Next, pour in both packs of cake mix and stir well.
  6. Now, get JoeySalads' urine and mix it in.
  7. Drop a couple cigarette butts in as well for aroma.
  8. Then, take JonTron's chewed banana and mix it together.
  9. After that, add in Jacksepticeye's pubic hair.
  10. Collect the toenail clippings from Makemebad35 and Anthony Fantano, and drop them into the bowl. (You may smell them before adding them in.)
  11. Get KSI's pubic hair and let it join the collection.
  12. Place Pewdiepie's butt hair into the bowl.
  13. Lastly pour in Vsauce's saliva and mix altogether.
  14. Now you'll want to portion out your batter into you 3 baking pans.
  15. Bake at 350F for an hour.
  16. Assemble your cake according to the image above and decorate with frosting and sprinkles.
  17. There you have it. A 3-tier cake perfect for any occasion!
