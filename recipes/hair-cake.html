Hair Cake

Description

This lovely cake recipe is one you'll have fun making with your friends! You and your pals will find this recipe entertaining and you'll be pleased with the final product so long as you follow with these steps:

Ingredients

  • Betty Crocker Super Moist Vanilla Cake Mix
  • Freshly Shaved-Off Hair (Substitute: Pet Fur)
  • 3 Beaten Eggs
  • Carton Of Eggs
  • Carton Of Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Alcohol (Of Your Preference)
  • Icing
  • Sprinkles
  • Mixing Bowl With 4 Smoked Cigarettes Inside (Ashes Included)
  • Baking Pan
  • Razor

Steps

  1. Pour in your beaten eggs over the cigarettes and ashes.
  2. Tear open your cake mix and shake the contents into the bowl.
  3. Grab two handfuls of flour and throw them into the bowl.
  4. Get your carton of eggs and take out three. Crack them on your friends' heads and drop the contents into the bowl.
  5. Next, get your carton of milk and drown the mixing bowl with it.
  6. Mix everything together with your hands.
  7. Scoop any excess off of your workstation back into the bowl. (For some extra fun, you can moan during this step!)
  8. While you admire your work, you might notice some hair in it already. Don't worry, here comes the fun part!
  9. Taste your cake batter and determine whose hair it is.(It should taste pretty good)
  10. Once the hair has been identified, restrain your friend, and shave their hair off into the bowl.
  11. Feed your friend alcohol to make the process easier.
  12. Once your friend is completely bald, grab a handful of butter and lather onto scalp.
  13. Optional: Use a razor to shave off any excess hair including eyebrows.
  14. Mix hair into the batter until it is nice and thick.
  15. For extra flavoring, collect your friends' beard, leg, nipple, and stomach hair and mix into bowl
  16. While mixing, add extra egg and flour as needed.
  17. Taste your batter at this stage, you'll find that it's very hard to swallow!
  18. Transfer your batter into the baking pan.
  19. Place in oven and cook for 50 minutes at 350F.
  20. Decorate with icing and sprinkles and youre done.
  21. Enjoy!

!Disclamer!

If your bald friend begins to have frequent nosebleeds, coughing up blood and/or weakness in lower body, Take them to the nearest hospital and get them checked for cancer.

Gagging while eating the cake is normal!
