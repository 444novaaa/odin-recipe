Vomit Cake

Description

This cake recipe is great for Birthdays, Holidays, or even as a gift for your significant other. You and whoever eats this cake will be gracious knowing that it was made with love from deep within~

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Self-Raising Flour
  • 1 Cup Castor Sugar
  • 1 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  • Vegetable Oil
  • 1/2 Cup Milk
  • 3 Eggs
  • Hefty scoop of Salt
  • Chocolate Syrup
  • Frosting
  • Sprinkles
  • Water
  • Funnel
  • Baking Pan

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350F.
  2. Start out by placing the funnel into your mouth.
  3. Next you'll want to funnel in your flour.
  4. Try your hardest to swallow it (The consumer will be able to taste your efforts.)
  5. You may drink water after swallowing.
  6. Then, funnel in your sugar and try to swallow.
  7. If the sugar doesn't dissolve quickly enough, you may drink water.
  8. Drink the vanilla extract. Do not be alarmed by the taste.
  9. Now chug your Vegetable Oil.
  10. You'll then want to take a hefty scoop of salt and consume it.
  11. Afterwards, take your 3 eggs, crack them, and whisk in seperate bowl.
  12. For this next step, you'll want to have your baking pan nearby.
  13. Once whisked, prepare your funnel and pour your eggs into your mouth.
  14. It is likely you will begin to vomit at this stage, that's normal! Make sure it goes into the baking pan.
  15. If you do not vomit, place your fingers at the back of your throat and trigger your gag reflexes until you do.
  16. You can drink your chocolate syrup for more flavor and to make the process more bearable.
  17. Vomit until the pan is at least halfway full to ensure all of your batter comes out.
  18. It tends to be watery at this stage, so add more flour until smooth consistency.
  19. Cook for 50 minutes, checking occasionally.
  20. Let it cool before decorating with frosting and sprinkles.
  21. Serve to your loved ones.
